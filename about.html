About Me

I’m Fredrick Mwendwa, a frontend developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I create digital experiences that are visually clean, technically robust, and easy to use. I enjoy turning ideas into functional interfaces and improving performance through efficient code and thoughtful design.

My Mission

To build digital products that feel fast, intuitive and inclusive — from lightweight mobile experiences to full-scale web applications.

My Journey

I started coding by designing small interfaces and sites for local shops in Machakos and Nairobi. Over time, I developed a strong foundation in JavaScript, learned modern frameworks like React and Vue, and grew into a full frontend engineer focused on scalability, performance, and clean architecture.
Today, I build components, dashboards, marketplaces, LMS systems and have collaborated with startups, agencies and global teams.

Let’s build something incredible together.

I’m open to freelance work, long-term contracts, or full-time roles.
